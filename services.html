Meet the Doctor
Hearing Tests

Comprehensive diagnostic audiology.

Accurate diagnosis is the first step to better hearing. All tests are conducted on state-of-the-art, ISO-standard instruments.

Pure Tone Audiometry

The gold-standard hearing test that maps the softest sounds you can hear across frequencies, producing your personal audiogram.

Impedance Audiometry

Evaluates middle-ear function — eardrum mobility, middle-ear pressure and acoustic reflexes — to locate the source of hearing difficulty.

Speech Audiometry

Measures how well you hear and understand speech, guiding realistic expectations and hearing aid programming.

Eustachian Tube Function Test

Assesses ventilation of the middle ear, important for pressure-related hearing problems and ear discomfort.

OAE Test

Otoacoustic emissions testing — a quick, objective check of inner-ear (cochlear) function, suitable even for infants.

Not sure which test you need?

Start with a free consultation — our audiologist will recommend the right diagnostic pathway for you.

Balance & Vertigo

Vestibular function testing.

Complete evaluation for balance disorders, dizziness and vertigo.

Videonystagmography (VNG)

Video-based evaluation of eye movements to diagnose balance disorders, dizziness and vertigo originating from the inner ear.

Electronystagmography (ENG)

Electrode-based assessment of the vestibular system for a complete picture of balance function.
