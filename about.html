About Hearing 360 Degree

A specialized organization dedicated to comprehensive hearing care — hearing tests, hearing aid sales, and lifelong after-sales support.

Who We Are

Care delivered by RCI-registered audiologists.

Hearing 360 Degree is a specialized organization dedicated to providing comprehensive hearing care services, including hearing tests, hearing aid sales, and after-sales support — delivered by a team of highly qualified, RCI-registered audiologists committed to the best possible outcomes for our clients.

Our hearing assessments are conducted using state-of-the-art, ISO-standard instruments, ensuring precise and reliable results. This rigorous approach allows us to accurately diagnose hearing issues and recommend the appropriate interventions.

To help you choose the right hearing aid, we offer a thorough trial process following a well-defined protocol, so the hearing aid you select is perfectly suited to your specific needs.

  • A leading hearing health care clinic for the hearing impaired
  • Team of highly qualified and experienced audiologists
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure with advanced hearing assessment equipment
  • Wide range of hearing aids with fitting & post-sale services
  • Comprehensive vestibular function testing for balance disorders, dizziness & vertigo
  • Premium partners: Signia, Phonak, ReSound, Coselgi, Starkey and many more

Hearing Solutions

A complete range, tailored to you.

Whether you're experiencing mild hearing loss or more severe issues, we have the right solution for you.

1. Hearing Aids

Behind-the-Ear (BTE), In-the-Ear (ITE), In-the-Canal (ITC) and Completely-in-Canal (CIC) — from powerful amplification to discreet designs.

2. Hearing Tests & Diagnostics

Comprehensive tests with advanced audiometric equipment to diagnose the type and degree of hearing loss accurately.

3. Custom Hearing Protection

Custom earmolds and earplugs for those exposed to loud environments — protection with comfort.

4. Tinnitus Management

Effective strategies and devices to reduce the perception of ringing or buzzing in the ears.

5. Assistive Listening Devices

Amplified telephones, TV listening systems and personal amplifiers to enhance hearing in every setting.

6. Accessories & Maintenance

Batteries, cleaning tools, remote controls — plus regular maintenance so your devices function optimally.

7. Telecare & Remote Support

Professional hearing care from the comfort of your home — remote adjustments and expert advice.
