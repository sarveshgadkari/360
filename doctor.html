Areas of Expertise

Who is going to treat you.

Hearing Diagnostics

Complete audiological evaluation — pure tone, impedance and speech audiometry, OAE and vestibular testing.

Hearing Aid Consultation

Expert, logic-first advice on the right model based on your audiogram, technology needs and lifestyle — never sales-driven.

Speech Therapy

Assessment and therapy for speech and language development in children, and speech clarity and fluency in adults.

Tinnitus Management

Structured strategies and devices to manage ringing or buzzing in the ears.

Balance & Vertigo

Vestibular evaluation (VNG/ENG) for dizziness, imbalance and vertigo complaints.

After-care & Follow-up

Regular reviews, hearing aid fine-tuning and family counselling for long-term hearing success.

The Consultation Philosophy

Honest guidance, always.

  • Consult with a qualified audiologist (with RCI registration) before any purchase
  • Complete your audiological evaluation first — the device follows the diagnosis
  • Understand the types of hearing aid and the logical reason behind every recommendation
  • Always take a hearing aid trial across real environments before deciding
  • Never accept illogical counselling — ask questions until you're convinced
  • Full care & maintenance guidance provided with every device
